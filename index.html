About Our Trust

Rooted in Faith, Driven by Compassion

Zu Al Noorain Trust Narwara (ZANT) is built on the pillars of Islamic charity. We aim to bridge the gap between those who want to give and those in dire need, ensuring every penny is spent with transparency and impact.

⭐
Zakat Management Properly calculated and distributed as per Sharia guidelines.
🤝
Community Support Local focus on Narwara, Eidgah and surrounding areas.
💎
Transparency We maintain complete records of every donation and beneficiary.
What We Do

Pillars of Giving

We specialize in three main areas of Islamic charity to ensure holistic support for our community.

01 🌙

Zakat

الزكاة

The obligatory charity that purifies your wealth. We identify the most deserving eligible recipients (Asnaf) to ensure your Zakat fulfills its divine purpose.

Zakat Calculator
02 🤲

Sadqat

الصدقة

Voluntary charity that wards off calamities and brings blessings. Your Sadqat helps us respond quickly to emergencies and daily hardships of the poor.

03 🌿

Khariyat

الخيرات

General welfare and goodness. This fund is used for community development, education support, and healthcare assistance for those who cannot afford it.
